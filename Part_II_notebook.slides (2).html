I chose the Prosper Loan dataset. (How Loan Original Amount is affected by Income range and Employment status)¶

Loan original amount is the amount of loan gotten by a borrower. The highest loans where given to people in the 100,000 and above income range who were employed.

(How High Prosper Scores and Employment Stataus affects Loan Original Amount)¶

I noticed that borrowers who were employed and had high prosper scores got the biggest loans around 0 - 16,000 dollars.

Borrowers who were employed but had lower prosper scores got lower loans that their counterparts with higher scores. Getting loans in the region of 0 - 8000 dollars which is 6000 dollars lower in range.

(How Income Range and Employment Status Affects BorrowerAPR)¶

I noticed from the plot below that borrowers within the lowest income rangers usually got higher interest on their loans regardless of employment status as their loans are considered more risky.
